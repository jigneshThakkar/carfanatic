Do You Need ADAS Calibration for Your Vehicle?

Ensuring that your Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) are properly calibrated is crucial for maintaining safety and performance.

Here are some situations when you may need ADAS Calibration with your auto body repairs:

  • Did your windshield or auto glass break and does it need replacement?
  • Was your vehicle recently in an accident that could have misaligned sensors?
  • Are you replacing cameras or sensors on your vehicle?
  • Does your vehicle need maintenance?
  • Have you recently had suspension repairs or modifications?

If your vehicle has suffered damage or you fit one of the upper mentioned causes, contact Auto Vizion. We have a reliable and skillful ADAS Calibration service. Our team works with a number of brands to use OEM parts whenever possible and to provide reputable repairs.

How We Works:

PRE SCAN

All vehicles undergo a complete diagnostic scan prior to any calibrations. This scan identifies any existing or nonrelated fault codes. Performing a diagnostic scan prior to calibration allows us to assess the vehicle in its current state and diagnose potential ADAS issues.

CALIBRATE

Once satisfied the vehicle is aligned, the calibration process can be undertaken. Multiple radars, lidars and cameras may require calibration. Fuel level and ride height affect calibration so we are sure validate the current status of these factors as part of the calibration process.

POST SCAN

An initial post calibration scan is conducted. No calibration is complete without the documentation to support it! We complete a post scan of the vehicle and provide you with reports validating all work that has been performed.

ROAD TEST

The vehicle is road tested to check for nonconformity and to ensure ADAS systems are operational.

FINAL SCAN

Upon return to site the vehicle then has a final diagnostic check to ensure no fault codes have activated during road test.

REPORT

A comprehensive report is supplied for each vehicle along with a certificate of calibration.
